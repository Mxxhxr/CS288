Craft your stories and preserve your memories with this quality single-color cardstock paper. This packet of high-quality colored cardstock paper is both acid and lignin free. Ideal for card making, matting your favorite photos or adding color to any project, this card paper is a great staple for your memorable creations.

Details:
  • Available in multiple colors
  • 8.5" x 11" (21.5cm x 27.9cm)
  • 65lb/176gsm
  • 50 sheets
  • Acid and lignin free
